Privacy Policy for Animoki SRL Products

Last updated: 2025/03/10

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all software programs, browser extensions, and digital products (collectively "Products") developed and distributed by Animoki SRL ("the Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

2. Data Collection

Our Products may collect and store data to provide and improve their functionality. The types of data we may collect include:

  • User Input: Information that you provide directly, such as names, email addresses, and other data entered into our Products.
  • Preferences and Settings: Information regarding your usage and configuration preferences, stored locally on your device or via browser storage mechanisms.
  • Technical Information: Data such as browser type, operating system, and diagnostic data, which help us optimize performance and troubleshoot issues.

3. Use of Data

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring proper functionality and automating processes within our Products.
  • Saving and retrieving your preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Communicating with external servers only when necessary for specific functionalities (for example, API calls to confirm transactions or updates).

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purposes beyond what is necessary for the operation and improvement of our Products.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data collected by our Products is primarily stored locally on your device or within secure storage provided by the platform (such as browser storage). When data is transmitted to external servers (e.g., for API calls), it is done over secure connections and only the minimal required information is transmitted.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • If required by law or in response to legal process.
  • If necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Animoki SRL, our users, or others.
  • For technical support or service improvements, where third-party service providers are involved; these providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are restricted from using your data for any other purposes.

6. Remote Code and External Communications

Our Products do not load or execute remote code in the user's browser. All functionalities are contained within the Product's code. Any communication with external servers (e.g., API calls) is strictly limited to essential functions, such as confirming transactions or retrieving updates, and is performed securely.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data collected about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of data collection; however, please note that some features may be limited as a result.

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
